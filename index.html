Sail Monitor

The project focuses on using AIS vessel tracking data to improve situational awareness and navigational safety for sailors and recreational boaters crossing the Atlantic. By analyzing global maritime traffic patterns, we can identify high-traffic zones, reduce collision risks, and support safer route planning in open-ocean environments.
